# The Router — choosing secondary voices by content type

*The **primary voice** (`kaue.md`) is always on; it sets the tone and is **not** counted toward the layer
budget. This router chooses which **secondary voice(s)** to lean on, by the piece's **dominant job**, not
its surface topic. Differentiation is **inherent to each voice's lane** (the scope header at the top of each
secondary profile), so there are **no author-vs-author special-case rules**.*

**Governing idea:** the primary voice already does a watered-down version of all five crafts (most congruent
with Helius). So routing selects an **amplifier**, not an identity. Pull the craft this *specific* piece most
needs, in Kaue's voice, and stop.

---

## 1. The five lanes (axes that don't fight for the same sentences)
Each secondary owns one axis; that's why two on the *same* axis must not co-lead, and why different axes
stack cleanly.

- **helius**, *structure & evidence*: walk-through → teardown in dataflow order; show the real artifact;
  quantify; claim+limit. Leads practical how-to / integration / reference; safe universal guest.
- **vitalik**, *derivation, sustained*: build the idea up from first principles; rule out naive fixes in
  tiers; steelman; name the tradeoffs and failure modes; keep deriving past the one-liner.
- **balaji**, *framing & scope*: extend one anchor analogy into its consequences; decompose a thesis into
  auditable pillars; lineage/inevitability. Leads broad/interconnected theses; also an opener-guest.
- **hayes**, *engagement & stamina*: organizing-metaphor spine, reader-stakes, roadmap/skim-path,
  forces-as-actors, falsifiable+actionable close. Native domain: macro / markets / geopolitics / tokenomics.
- **hotz**, *compression & collapse*: collapse to "the best model of X is Y"; order-of-magnitude argument;
  one portable line; then stop, only when an honest collapse exists.

## 2. Per-lane triggers & anti-triggers

### helius — practical structure & evidence
**Lead when:** how-to / tutorial / integration; reference explainer of an *existing, documented* system; the
artifact *is* the lesson (struct, instruction, CPI, data); troubleshooting / debugging / post-mortems;
security exploit-class walkthroughs (vulnerable vs patched code); mixed-audience pieces needing gated depth.
**Don't lead when:** the piece is persuasive/speculative/thesis-driven (no artifact to show) or broad and
conceptual. There Helius **demotes to the evidence layer.** But its hygiene (quantify, gloss, claim+limit)
stays on every piece.

### vitalik — first-principles derivation
**Lead when:** "why is it designed this way?" for something non-obvious; genuinely contested tradeoffs
deserving fair adjudication (modular vs monolithic, optimistic vs ZK, PoS nuances); mechanism-design /
cryptoeconomics where the reasoning chain is the product; **anything that needs failure-modes / tradeoffs /
staged derivation to be correct** (incl. *why a security check is necessary*, and *deriving a mechanism that
happens to be economic*, Vitalik backbone, Hayes guest).
**Don't lead when:** the topic is simple/settled and just needs showing (→ helius); hype/motivational content
(goes flat, fights Kaue's edge-energy).

### balaji — framing, scope & lineage (a full backbone for breadth)
**Lead when:** broad/interconnected "big-picture" pieces where many parts must cohere into one frame; a
bold/contestable thesis a skeptic will resist (the "scaling ladder" earns it incrementally); content that
benefits from historical lineage / proof-by-pattern; "why couldn't this exist before?" pieces. **Also** an
opener-guest on a narrower piece (set the anchor-analogy + lineage up front, then hand off).
**Don't lead when:** a narrow concrete how-to or single-mechanism explainer (it would inflate into
civilizational preamble). **"Grandiose" is NOT a trigger, *structural breadth* is.** Keep Kaue's altitude
humble: use Balaji's *architecture*, not his oracular register.

### hayes — engagement & stamina
**Lead when:** **economics / markets / geopolitics / tokenomics / crowd-psychology** (his native domain:
recast impersonal forces as "an actor doing a thing with a motive"; *holds regardless of length*); the piece
is **long / intimidating** and reader *abandonment* is the main risk; cold/general audiences needing a story
+ stakes + skim-path; any analytical piece that should **end on "what to watch / what would prove me wrong /
what to do now."**
**Don't lead when:** short, dense, expert-facing reference (scaffolding is pure overhead → helius); a rigorous
derivation where a vivid metaphor would *replace* the mechanism (→ vitalik leads, Hayes guests at the seams).
**Hard guard:** never let trader-cynicism leak. Kaue is bullish-on-tech, honest-on-risk.

### hotz — reductive demystification
**Lead when:** a topic is hyped / jargon-cloaked / made to sound harder than it is, and a *single clean
reframe dissolves it honestly* ("rent is a refundable deposit, not a fee"); fighting a widespread misframing;
short, punchy myth-busters; second-order-effects.
**Don't lead when:** the topic genuinely resists collapse — **forcing "X is just Y" when it needs an asterisk
ships a confidently wrong claim.** If the reframe needs a caveat, it's a vitalik derivation instead. **This is
why security never leads here.** The eject is built into hotz's own scope header. No external rule needed.

## 3. Stacking rules + the cap
- **One voice owns the backbone** (global structure); others contribute **local moves** at specific positions.
  They don't re-architect.
- **Stack only across different lanes; never two voices on the same axis.** In particular **vitalik + hotz
  must not co-lead** (laborious derivation + abrupt collapse of the same point = incoherence). A single hotz
  portable-line close on a vitalik piece is fine as a *guest* move.
- **Safe pairs:** hayes-backbone + helius-evidence (long economic pieces); vitalik-backbone + helius-artifact
  (rigorous mechanism explainers); vitalik-backbone + hayes-guest (economic mechanisms); balaji-backbone +
  helius/vitalik-evidence (broad theses that still need a concrete anchor); hotz-backbone + helius-specifics
  (short demystifiers).
- **Helius is the universal stack-mate** (its evidence axis is unoccupied; layer it almost for free).
- **THE CAP:** budget = primary (free, always on, **not** counted) + backbone (1) + guest (≤1) = **at most 2
  secondary layers.** 3 only for 3,000+ word pieces with physically segregated sections; a wish for a third is
  usually a signal to **split the piece.** Past two layers the primary voice's human seam and uneven texture
  get crowded out — and that's what makes it Kaue.

## 4. Failure modes (ranked by damage)
1. **Hotz on an irreducible topic → confidently WRONG.** Detonates "don't trust, verify." Route hotz only
   when an honest one-liner exists; needs an asterisk → vitalik. **On security this ships an exploit — default
   security to helius or vitalik.**
2. **Hayes trader-cynicism on a markets piece → un-Kaue tone.** His highest-frequency domain is where his
   worst failure lurks. Enforce affectionate builder-optimism; run "crisis-as-opportunity" as the antidote.
3. **Balaji altitude on a how-to → oracular bloat.** Balaji leads *breadth*, not procedure; never let it
   inflate a narrow mechanism explainer into civilizational preamble.
4. **Vitalik on a simple/settled topic → flat, false-depth.** Reads "studied, not lived."
5. **Helius backbone on a persuasive piece → tidy teardown of nothing** (no argument engine).
6. **Over-stacking (3+) → the human seam gets crowded out** (insidious; every pick looks defensible).

**Meta-failure across all six:** any route that makes the body *uniformly polished and evenly enthusiastic.*
Every craft, applied too thoroughly, *smooths*, so the router's last guardrail is **dosage**: pull craft at
the positions that need it, leave routine stretches plain and a little loose.

## 5. The decision procedure (end to end)
1. **Always-on:** the Kaue primary voice, sets tone, owns the seams and the uneven texture (free, uncounted).
2. **Classify the dominant job** → pick the lead voice by lane (SKILL.md table). Default *expository* →
   helius; troubleshooting → helius (+guest); macro/economics/markets → hayes; deep derivation / "why this
   design" → vitalik; broad interconnected thesis → balaji; honest "X is just Y" demystifier → hotz; security
   → helius/vitalik (never hotz); **pure motivation / course-opener → no backbone, the primary carries it +
   ≤1 light guest.**
3. **Add ≤1 guest on a different lane** (§3); helius is the safe universal guest; never vitalik+hotz co-lead.
4. **Apply the universal good-teaching baseline regardless:** quantify with units, name every cost, staged
   difficulty, bounded analogy, just-in-time definitions, strip secondary idiolect.
5. **Cap at 2 secondary layers** (primary free; backbone + guest); 3 only for 3,000+ words, segregated; else split.
6. **Hold each used voice's guardrail, and protect the seam + uneven texture above all:** craft at positions,
   routine stretches plain.

**Bottom line:** route on the **dominant job, not the topic.** Each lane's scope header makes the choice
self-evident; the two closest pairs resolve by scope (helius *shows* vs vitalik *derives*; vitalik *keeps
deriving* vs hotz *collapses and stops*). Stack across lanes, cap at two, and protect the uneven human seam
above all craft.
