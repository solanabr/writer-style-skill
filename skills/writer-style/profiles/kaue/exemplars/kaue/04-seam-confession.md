A very recurrent problem on hackathons is spending too much time shaping your idea while the deadline keeps getting closer and closer. I've been guilty of that, big time, so trust me — pinning down your value proposition less than 24h before the pitch, knowing only now you can actually code away, is not a productive scenario.
